About

Nation Infinity Africa is a foundation that takes care of about 180 children, providing accommodation facilities and other basic needs of life. The foundation offers opportunities for various activities such as metal skills, acrobatics, gymnastics, comedy, music, dance, and other programs like guidance counseling, computer training, leadership training, and environmental conservation plus protection.

Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every individual has access to opportunities, regardless of their socioeconomic background. We aim to foster a global community that values diversity, equity, and sustainability.

Values

  • Integrity: We operate with transparency and hold ourselves accountable to the highest ethical standards.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and strive for innovative solutions.
  • Collaboration: We work hand-in-hand with local and international stakeholders.
  • Community Focus: We prioritize the needs of the communities we serve.
