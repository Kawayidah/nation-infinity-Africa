Mission

Empowering poor orphans and street children, through talent and skills development, provide acess to formal and informal education plus good acommodation facility in order to enable them reacch their full potential.

What's New

New Education Initiative Launched

We are excited to announce the launch of our new education program aimed at providing quality learning resources to children in remote areas. This initiative will support thousands of students in gaining access to necessary educational tools and opportunities.

Health Campaign for Rural Areas

Our latest health campaign focuses on improving healthcare access and awareness in rural communities. Through mobile clinics and health workshops, we aim to provide essential medical services and health education to those in need.

Environmental Sustainability Projects

We have launched several new projects aimed at promoting environmental sustainability. These include tree planting drives, waste management programs, and community clean-up events to protect and preserve our natural resources.
